Requirements vary by program: Golden Visas typically require AED 2M+ in real estate or AED 10M+ in business investment. Property investor visas generally require AED 750K-1M depending on the emirate. We analyze your profile to recommend the most cost-effective and suitable residency pathway.

Dubai offers several tax-efficient structures including free zone companies (0% corporate tax), offshore companies (tax-neutral), and strategic use of double tax treaties. We design optimal structures based on your asset types, investment strategies, and cross-border considerations to maximize tax efficiency while ensuring full compliance.

Family office setup involves entity selection (free zone vs mainland), governance framework creation, investment policy development, succession planning, and regulatory compliance. We handle the complete process including residency for family members, banking relationships, and integration with existing international structures.
