BUSINESS STRUCTURING OPTIONS

Choosing the Right
Business Entity

Mainland Companies

Trade directly within UAE market, local sponsor required (51% ownership), access to all business activities, ideal for businesses targeting local customers and government contracts.

Free Zone Companies

100% foreign ownership, tax benefits, streamlined setup, limited direct UAE trading, ideal for international businesses, consulting firms, and holding companies.

Offshore Companies

Asset protection, international business, no physical office required, cannot trade within UAE, ideal for holding assets, international trading, and wealth management.
