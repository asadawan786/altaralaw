Company setup timelines vary based on business activity, location (mainland vs free zone), and structure. Typically, free zone setups take 1-3 weeks, while mainland companies require 2-4 weeks. We provide clear timelines from day one and handle all authority coordination to ensure efficient processing.

Mainland companies can trade directly within the UAE and internationally but require a local sponsor (51% ownership). Free zones offer 100% foreign ownership, tax benefits, and streamlined setup but limit direct trading within the UAE. We analyze your business model to recommend the optimal structure for your goals.
